Designed for a premium medical and aesthetics conference at Centara Grand, this exhibition pavilion focuses on structural transparency and clear brand hierarchy. The booth uses an open, double-sided layout framed by a matte black architectural structure, contrasting sharply against a warm, light timber floor to create an inviting yet clinical environment.

The main feature wall is split between a high-brightness product lightbox and a clean white branding surface that highlights the "Alma" and "Soprano Titanium" identity. Functionally, the footprint maximizes every square meter by integrating dual product demonstration counters with built-in storage, a freestanding digital display totem for dynamic media, and an intimate consultation corner with minimalist white seating.
