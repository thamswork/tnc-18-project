This project focuses on a modular, removable booth structure designed for quick assembly and relocation. The setup relies on a sleek white grid backdrop that allows for flexible display arrangements, while a custom pink neon sign provides strong brand recognition against the neutral frames.

The service counter blends the brand's signature pink palette with clean white surfaces, organizing the space into an accessible ordering corner and a streamlined product storage zone underneath. To anchor the display, a freestanding digital menu screen is placed at the front to capture visitor attention, making the entire system highly adaptable for short-term events, markets, or temporary mall placements.
