Designed for the high-traffic Beacon Zone at Central World, this open kiosk maximizes a central island footprint to ensure 360-degree brand visibility. The structure uses the brand's recognizable pink palette, anchored by a smooth, curved service counter with integrated neon base lighting that highlights the kiosk's silhouette against the mall flooring.

To balance the open floor plan, an overhead canopy features warm vertical timber slats and prominent backlit signage, creating a defined sense of space without blocking sightlines. The layout is optimized for quick service, neatly housing a curved pastry showcase, clear overhead digital menus, and dedicated customer touchpoints while maintaining a clean, cohesive storefront.
