Located in one of Bangkok’s most prestigious neighborhoods, this penthouse project focuses on a "Modern Neoclassical" theme. The design utilizes a monochromatic palette of warm off-whites and creams to create a sense of expansive, airy luxury.

Architectural Details: Custom wall paneling and crown moldings provide a sophisticated backdrop throughout the living and bedroom areas.

Gourmet Kitchen: A spacious, symmetry-focused kitchen featuring integrated appliances, a rolling library ladder for high-ceiling storage, and a central island.

Master Suite: A seamless blend of wellness and rest, featuring an in-room fitness area and a spa-like bathroom finished in floor-to-ceiling marble with a classic clawfoot tub.

Materiality: The use of light oak flooring, polished marble, and champagne gold hardware creates a cohesive, high-end feel that maximizes natural light.
