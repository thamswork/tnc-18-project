The design for Irvin Thai Kitchen 1976 embraces an authentic, heritage-inspired aesthetic that blends into the historic character of the Bang Rak district. The facade is defined by rich, warm timber paneling, traditional window frames, and full-length glass panes that offer a welcoming view inside. Strategically placed warm overhead spots and an illuminated exterior sign highlight the shop's classic branding.
