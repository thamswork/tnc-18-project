The Rolling Pinn’s presence at Slow Combo was designed as a strategic touchpoint to engage with a younger, creative demographic in the Samyan area.

Key Highlights:

* Visual Identity: The space utilizes bold neon signage and the brand’s signature pink palette to create an "Instagrammable" moment, driving organic social media reach.
* Operational Efficiency: Despite the limited 15 sq.m. area, the layout was optimized for high-volume grab-and-go service, featuring a curated selection of best-selling cookies and cakes.
* Community Integration: The pop-up acted as a lifestyle extension, aligning the brand with Slow Combo’s focus on energy, art, and community wellness.
* This project successfully bridged the gap between a traditional bakery and a modern lifestyle brand, ensuring brand consistency across temporary retail formats.
