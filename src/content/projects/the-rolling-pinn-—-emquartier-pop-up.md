Fitting a full retail experience into just 4 sqm required a vertical strategy. We maintained the brand's signature pink identity while integrating a full-height chiller, a curved pastry display, and a high-brightness digital screen within a single modular unit. The overhead signage provides strong visibility from a distance, making this tiny footprint a powerful point of sale in high-traffic luxury zones.
