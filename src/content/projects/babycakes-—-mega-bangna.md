For the Mega Bangna location, the design pivots to a distinct monochromatic periwinkle blue palette contrasted by playful yellow typography. The elongated island layout stretches efficiently along the mall floor, integrating a low-profile service bar, built-in display cases for cakes, and flush vertical digital menus at both ends for clear customer onboarding.

Adjacent to the main counter, the footprint expands to include a dedicated seating section. This area features a matching periwinkle bench paired with minimalist metallic round tables, creating a self-contained dining experience within the open retail corridor.
