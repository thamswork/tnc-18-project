For the ICONSIAM location, the design adapts to a high-traffic mall corridor with a smooth, curved counter layout. The kiosk uses the brand's signature pink palette but introduces a rounded architectural silhouette that guides customers naturally toward the central bakery display and ordering point.

Bright neon signage and integrated under-counter lighting give the pavilion a distinct glow within the open retail floor. Despite the compact footprint, the design successfully balances brand impact with practical service space, integrating full-height storage, a digital display screen, and clear product presentation that catches the eye of passing shoppers
