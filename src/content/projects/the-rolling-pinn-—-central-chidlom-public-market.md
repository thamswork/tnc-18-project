For the Central Chidlom Public Market location, the design adapts to a shared food hall environment by focusing on a front-facing counter setup. The layout features a large, glass-fronted refrigeration unit built directly into the counter to showcase cakes and pastries clearly at eye level.

To maintain the brand's identity within the market stall structure, we utilized soft pink interior wall finishes, bright downlighting, and elevated floating shelves in red to add contrast and display packaged merchandise. The ordering station is seamlessly integrated on the right side with a illuminated display top, creating an efficient workflow for staff while keeping the presentation clean and organized for customers.
