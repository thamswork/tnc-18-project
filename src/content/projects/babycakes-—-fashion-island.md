Designed for the B1 food zone at Fashion Island, this kiosk translates the brand's distinct playful identity into a highly efficient retail setup. The space features smooth, curved counters and a vibrant pastel color palette that ensures high visibility from the surrounding walkways.

The layout prioritizes a smooth customer journey, centering around a low-profile glass display unit that presents the signature mini cake trays directly at eye level. To balance the compact footprint, we utilized minimalist overhead framing and integrated soft, diffused lighting, creating an inviting, photo-friendly storefront that maximizes storage without cluttering the service area.
