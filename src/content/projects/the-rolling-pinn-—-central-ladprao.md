This compact kiosk maximizes its 18 sqm footprint with an all-pink aesthetic and classic architectural details. The design features custom lattice paneling, integrated digital menus, and a glass-fronted bakery display. We used a monochromatic palette to create a high-visibility, "Instagrammable" landmark within the mall, finished with a cozy sit-down nook for customers.
