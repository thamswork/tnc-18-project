For the Siam Paragon location, the design maximizes a busy food hall footprint with a high-visibility, 360-degree open kiosk. The layout uses a smooth, curved pink counter accented by bright pink under-lighting to draw in foot traffic from the surrounding aisles.

To stand out in a crowded environment, we designed a striking overhead structure featuring vertical wooden slats and prominent backlit branding. The space efficiently packs in a curved glass display for pastries, integrated menu boards, and a dedicated ordering station, all while maintaining a warm and approachable storefront for customers.
