## What Is a Commercial Fit-Out?

A fit-out is the process of making an interior space ready for occupation — transforming a shell or an existing space into a functional, branded environment. This includes partitioning, flooring, ceilings, lighting, joinery, and all finishes.

TNC Build Studio handles fit-outs for offices, retail stores, showrooms, clinics, and hospitality spaces across Bangkok and the wider Thailand market.

## Office Fit-Outs: Beyond the Open Plan

The open-plan office had its moment. What we're seeing now is a more nuanced approach: zones designed for different types of work — focused individual work, collaborative sessions, informal conversation, and client-facing meetings — each with appropriate acoustic treatment, lighting levels, and furniture.

We design offices that people actually want to come to. That's not a small thing in a city where talent has options.

## Retail Fit-Outs: Where Brand Meets Commerce

A retail fit-out is one of the highest-stakes design exercises a brand undertakes. Every material, fixture, and lighting choice communicates something about the product and the brand's positioning.

We approach retail fit-outs with a strong commercial discipline — understanding target customer behaviour, average transaction value, and the role of the physical store in an omnichannel strategy.

## Key Considerations for Bangkok Commercial Fit-Outs

### Venue Regulations

Mall landlords in Bangkok (Central Pattana, Siam Piwat, Mall Group) each have their own technical requirements, approved material lists, and build windows. We have established relationships with all major venue management teams and handle all compliance documentation.

### Build Windows

Retail fit-outs in operating malls typically happen overnight or during restricted hours. We staff accordingly and have never missed a handover date.

### Future Flexibility

Good commercial design anticipates change. We build in flexibility — moveable partitions, modular shelving systems, accessible cable management — so the space can evolve without a full refurbishment.

## Timeline

A typical 100–200 sqm commercial fit-out runs four to six weeks from design sign-off to handover. Smaller spaces and simpler briefs can be delivered faster.
