## Why Booth Design Matters More Than Ever

Thailand's trade show and exhibition calendar is packed — BITEC, IMPACT Arena, and Queen Sirikit National Convention Center host hundreds of events each year. With dozens of competing brands on the same floor, your booth has approximately three seconds to stop a visitor mid-stride.

At TNC Build Studio, we've learned that the booths that perform best are never the biggest — they're the most intentional.

## The Three Pillars of an Effective Exhibition Booth

### 1. Brand Clarity at a Distance

Before a visitor reads a single word, they should already feel your brand. This is achieved through silhouette, lighting, and colour — in that order.

We design booth structures with strong vertical presence so they read clearly across a crowded hall. A well-placed backlit graphic at 3–4 metres height draws the eye from 20 metres away. We then layer in brand colours and materials at eye level for closer engagement.

### 2. Purposeful Traffic Flow

A booth without a clear entry point bleeds visitors. We plan circulation paths the way retail designers plan shop floors — guiding visitors from an open, welcoming threshold through to a product zone, and finally to a consultation or closing area.

For clients like Alma and Profhilo, we designed exhibition pavilions where the journey through the space mirrored the brand's clinical-to-luxurious positioning.

### 3. Build Quality That Photographs Well

In 2026, your booth lives on social media long after the event ends. We select materials — frosted acrylic, brushed aluminium, fabric tension systems — that hold up under both harsh venue lighting and smartphone cameras.

## Our Process: Concept to Completion in 3 Weeks

Most exhibition projects run on compressed timelines. Our standard workflow:

- **Week 1** — Briefing, concept sketches, 3D visualisation
- **Week 2** — Material sourcing, fabrication begins, client sign-off on visuals
- **Week 3** — Fabrication completion, venue logistics, installation

We manage the full build in-house, which eliminates the coordination delays that come with using separate design and construction vendors.

## What to Budget for an Exhibition Booth in Bangkok

Booth costs in Thailand vary widely based on size, material quality, and complexity. As a general guide:

- **Shell scheme upgrade** (9–18 sqm): ฿80,000–฿200,000
- **Custom modular booth** (18–36 sqm): ฿250,000–฿600,000
- **Full custom pavilion** (36 sqm+): ฿700,000 and above

These figures include design, fabrication, and installation. Storage and dismantling can be quoted separately.
